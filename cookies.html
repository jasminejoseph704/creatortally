Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Creator Tally ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our application.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your overall experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Service to function properly. They enable core features such as:

  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Maintaining your logged-in state
  • Managing cookie consent preferences

Without these cookies, certain features would not work properly.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization, such as:

  • Remembering your dashboard settings
  • Storing your income tracking preferences
  • Remembering the month you're currently viewing

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies to understand how you use our Service. These cookies collect information about:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you navigate through the Service
  • Any errors or issues you encounter

This information helps us improve our Service and understand user behavior.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device. These may include:

Stripe

If you subscribe to our Pro tier, Stripe may set cookies to process payments securely and remember your payment preferences. For more information, visit Stripe's Cookie Policy.

Analytics Providers

We may use analytics tools that set their own cookies. These tools help us understand how users interact with our Service.

Your Cookie Choices

You have several options for managing cookies:

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Service, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preferences will be saved for future visits.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from all or certain websites
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Clear all cookies when you close your browser

Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our Service.

Device Settings

You may also manage cookies through your device's privacy settings.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Please revisit this page regularly to stay informed about our use of cookies.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us:

  • By visiting our website: https://creatortally.app
